Class AntWolf
In: ruby/ant_wolf.rb
Parent: AntAnimal

Wolves are controlled in a pack by a PackLeader. Typically wolves are resting. When a member gets too much hunger (<0.5), then the whole pack moves on to find some food (sheep or men)
